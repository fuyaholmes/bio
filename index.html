Albums and Mixtapes



Stokeley Clevon Goulbourne

Ski Mask was born as Stokeley Clevon Goulbourne on April 18, 1996 in Broward County, Florida. He is of Jamaican and Afro-American descent. As a kid, he found solace in music, and was a big fan of the rappers Fifty Cent and Logic. While growing up, he also liked to listen to Wu-Tang Clan, Lil Wayne, Missy Elliott, Busta Rhymes and Nelly. In 2014, while Ski Mask was at a detention center, he became friends with Florida rapper XXXTenacion. During that time, he discovered his talent for rapping, and when he was released, he formed a rap group named Very Rare with XXXTenacion.

Ski Mask has several hits to his credit including ‘Life Is Short’ that has over 6 million plays and ‘Take a Step Back’, which features XXXTenacion. As of March 2018, his SoundCloud page has over 622k followers and 54 tracks.

The way his career graph is progressing, critics believe that this young rapper would soon outgrow the internet landscape and venture into mainstream rap.
